  4  vs.   1

Chris Speer's Oakland A's defeated Kevin Gordon's Baltimore Orioles, via netplay, in 5 games.  Game 1 was a 13 inning thriller won by the A's 3-1.  The Orioles, who led the CBL in team batting, managed to only hit .201 against the A's in this series.  Barry Zito, Mark Mulder, and Jason Giambi were simply awesome for the A's.

  2 vs.    4

The Giants out homered the Cubs 7-6 but were held to a .203 BA by the Cubs hurlers, while the Cubs hit .283 in the series.  The Cubs 2.89 ERA was almost 2 points better than the Giants 4.67.  While Sosa and Bonds both hit .333 and drove in 7 and 6 runs respectively.  Randy Johnson's 2 wins and 22 K's in 15 innings along with a 1.80 ERA earned him MVP honors.  It was a very exciting and tense series as the Giants put up a much better fight than either manager expected.
