First baseman Josh Bell left the yard and had 4 RBI as the Pittsburgh Pirates
beat the Chicago (NL) Cubs by a score of 7 to 5.
 
Merrill Kelly(1-0) struggled at times, but was good enough to get the victory.
He gave up 5 runs, 9 hits and 2 walks in 6 innings and served up 2 'big
flies'.  Pittsburgh had 9 hits for the night.  
 
Brandon Workman recorded his 1st save.  Anibal Sanchez(1-1) was tagged with
the loss.  He allowed Pittsburgh 7 runs in 6 and 1/3 innings of work.  
 
'I was a little good, a little bad and a little ugly.' said Kelly, 'I was just
giving us a chance to win.'
